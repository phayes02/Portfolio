This part of the page covers Graphic Design projects that I have done here at HLG. In this class we were to create a company that we would create designs for. My company was more of a joke company called Banna.inc, as you can guess we sell bananas.

Website Mockup

This is a website mockup of our company website. We were to only create the homepage of the site and include the following item; navigation, logo, 4+ photos, contact form, promote a product or service, contact info, and social media icons.

Navigation: It's just a normal navigation bar of a website. There is the store, services, contact, shopping cart, and a search bar.

Promotion: Our promotion is an exclusive banana. In collabration with Marvel Studios we introduce the Incredible Hulk Banana. The text underneath got small for some reason but this is what it says, "*Order at Your OWN Risk. We are not responsible for any uncontrolled anger or destruction.*"

Store: This is the store section of the website. Here we sell our products (bananas) in a personal order or bulk amount. Each crate holds 64 bananas and they come at $5 per crate. The person who did these prices did not do a good job. 64 Bananas for $5?

Contact: Finally we have the contact section. Here you can contact the company by filling our a form or calling our phone service.

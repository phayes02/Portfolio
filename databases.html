SQL Queries

1st Query: In this query the use of a JOIN function is used. In this instance, the names of the shows and their producers (reality_show.showName, producer.compName) will be displayed. Using the table of producer we are pulling data where the ID of the producer is the same in the reality_show table.

2nd Query: This query is here to find reality shows that have a higher rating than 8. Selecting the name of the show (showName) from our reality_show table, we use the condition of where the ratingID in the rating table where the rating is higher than 8.

3rd Query: The last query is a conditional statement. Here we select the ID of the show (tvID) from the television table, where the location of the head office (head_office) is in Pittsburg.

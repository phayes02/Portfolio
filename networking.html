Lab Improvements

In our Networking course, we all had to make improvements to our lab. I was tasked to fix the Ethernet ports that broken. One of the previous students left behind a topology that displayed which ports were working and those that weren't. Even though I had that topology I still went around with a laptop and plugged into every single port. Although there were certain ones I did not mess with as other CS students already used them and I did not want to interfere with their connections. After figuring out the bad ports I took them out of their place and replaced their headers to see if that was the fix. For a majority, in which I only had three bad ports, that worked for them but there was one port that still would not work. After spending a lot of time trying to figure out what it could be, while looking at the CS Lab Documentation I noticed that the cable was plugged into the wrong port on the switch. Switching the port the cable worked perfectly fine.
